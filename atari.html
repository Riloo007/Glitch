October 29th, 2022

The Atari 400 was one of my earliest coding adventures. I spent many hours firmly pressing the plastic keys to try out programs written by others, and even longer writing my own programs. Unfortunately, I was one day plugging it in and stuck the power into the device port (used for external printers, input devices, etc.). Doing made a loud pop, and the computer never breathed again.
