November 12, 2022

Animation is extremely tedious. Right from age 8, I was making stop motion videos of lego, clay, and rooms that cleaned themselves. Then in high school, I took an animation course where I learned about the 12 principles of animation by Ollie Johnston and Frank Thomas.

Squash and Stretch
Timing
Anticipation
Staging
Follow Through and Overlapping Action
Straight Ahead and Pose to Pose
Slow in and Slow out
Arcs
Exaggeration
Secondary Action
Solid Drawing
Appeal

Any good animation uses some or all of these principles. Unfortunately, my early projects used little to none of them.
